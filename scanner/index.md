        <h1>Burp Scanner</h1>
        <p>
            Burp Scanner is a tool for performing automated scans of web sites, to discover content and audit for vulnerabilities.
        </p>
        <p>
            The work involved in performing a scan comprises two key phases:
        </p>
        <ul>
            <li>
                <strong><a href="crawling.html"> Crawling for content</a></strong> - This involves navigating around the application, following links, submitting forms, and logging in where necessary, to catalog the content of the application and the navigational paths within it. <a href="crawling.html" class="chevron-after">Read more</a>
            </li>
            <li>
                <strong><a href="auditing.html"> Auditing for vulnerabilities</a></strong> - This involves analyzing the application's traffic and behavior to identify security vulnerabilities and other issues. Depending on the scan configuration, it may involve sending a large number of requests to the application. <a href="auditing.html" class="chevron-after">Read more</a>
            </li>
        </ul>
